### OUR Story

As the little sister of Moro, Morito is the noisier, more rebellious sibling, eager to experiment and explore.  

We have two Moritos - one on Exmouth Market next to Moro and the other Hackney Road, one little and one vast, both trapping the vibrancy of London whilst transporting you to the Mediterranean. Tapas and mezze, cocktails and low intervention wines, soft drinks and music, light and hiding in the dark, our varied and mad team, orange counter and blue wall, and this extraordinary city that supports us. 

Welcome to the special atmosphere that is Morito - "it hits you like a wall of joy".

Sam and Sam Clark
