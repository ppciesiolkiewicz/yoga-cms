**About Exmouth Market**

“Exmouth Market might be less than 20 minutes by bus from the scrum of Tottenham Court Road but its laid-back charm means that it might as well be in a different town altogether. Its story is a classic tale of urban gentrification: a run-down area whose old warehouses were colonised by publishing, design and architecture firms in the 1990s.

Twenty years later, it is one of east London’s most desirable streets. It still has a trendy vibe, as demonstrated by the variety of stylish independent businesses lining the pedestrianised street, including some of the city’s most appealing restaurants and places to drink.” _– Metro_
