The wood-fired oven lies at the heart of the Moro kitchen, where our roast dishes take centre stage every Sunday. Segovian suckling pig, Pyrenean milk-fed lamb, Galician beef and Sutton Hoo chicken emerge from the oven alongside whole bream, seasonal roast vegetables and our renowned sourdough bread.

Our **Sunday Lunch Club** celebrates the spirit of the traditional Sunday feast — generous, convivial and designed for sharing. The menu begins with a selection of starters for the table, followed by spectacular roasts from the wood oven served with seasonal sides, and finishes with dessert.

Join us for a relaxed Sunday gathering and settle in for a proper feast.
