#### **NIR (Non-Intrusive Red Light) Therapy YOGA & Pilates at SHA-LA**

Red light therapy (RLT) utilizes specific wavelengths of red (approximately 630–660 nm) and near-infrared light (around 810–850 nm) to stimulate mitochondrial activity, increase cellular energy (ATP) production, and support tissue repair and recovery. At SHA-LA, we incorporate this evidence-based therapy into targeted yoga classes, combining the physiological benefits of movement with enhanced cellular regeneration and recovery.

_We offer red light therapy in two ways:_

group classes (restorative & flow sessions on our schedule) and as 1:1 add-ons (to complement other treatments).
