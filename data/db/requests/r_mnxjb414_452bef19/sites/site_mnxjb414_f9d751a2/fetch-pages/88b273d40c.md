## MLA Approved Locksmiths You Can Trust

London Locks is proud to be an [approved company member](https://www.locksmiths.co.uk/find-a-locksmith/london-locks-ltd/) of the Master Locksmiths Association (MLA) – the UK’s leading locksmith governing body.

The [Master Locksmiths Association](https://www.google.com/search?client=safari&rls=en&q=Master+Locksmiths+Association&ie=UTF-8&oe=UTF-8&sei=fe-7afmNEv6lhbIPwMTj-Qs&dlnr=1&mstk=AUtExfC7FTsR1WPw9_PyUYk7aq-urzOleN482lo2__S0kISyoUee0foqPgdcN9pyMxqV_zjCKVK1Gaw8TsCwmLPAfRr6fPeYL3Sw71aSrICS90YeELLILhUSBAhK2x8iHig80mGv9iGwex_55a7k1FF0_QOKQysnAar3dopjrKy7q7OWd_2YgAIH_nWso3KQcB-iFs9U1dCTXmFMgQFxEf4coab378NcJd49YS9fwdSdWD9dfI89jE7vOVHSCbVskA0BhXiALfRJRcF28nJcLV0QmrQO&csui=3&ved=2ahUKEwiyzrXb_quTAxVHXUEAHQ6-GJsQgK4QegQIARAB) (MLA) is the largest non-for profit trade association in the UK, established in 1958 to promote standards of excellence, skill, and integrity within the security industry.

It vets, inspects, and qualifies members, often acting as the authoritative body for police and government. London Locks are proudly the only MLA approved and accredited company based in Tower Hamlets, London.

Choosing an MLA-approved locksmith means:

*   Fully vetted and DBS-checked professionals
*   Regularly inspected work to ensure high standards
*   Up-to\-date training on modern locks and security systems
*   Trusted and recognised industry accreditation

This gives our customers complete peace of mind that they are working with a qualified and reputable locksmith company in East London. Having a brick and mortar Locksmith shop also gives our customers confidence over mobile London locksmith providers who are generally untraceable should anything go wrong.

We give 12 warranty with all our parts and our labour so you can be safe in the knowledge we won’t let you down should anything fail.
