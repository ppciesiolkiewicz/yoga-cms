## Our residential lock change services  

Changing your door locks is one of the simplest ways to **restore security and control access to your home**. Whether you’ve just moved in, lost a key, or had a recent security scare, we’ll make sure your doors are **properly secured before we leave**.

We help residential customers across **London, the Home Counties, and up to Huntingdon**.

![](https://wdlocksmiths.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/6.webp)

### uPVC door lock changes

*   Replace **uPVC multipoint locks**
    
*   Change **euro cylinders** (standard or anti-snap options)
    
*   Fix stiff handles or doors that won’t lock smoothly
    
*   Suitable for **front doors, back doors and patio doors**
    

### Home door lock replacement

*   Lock changes for **timber, composite and aluminium doors**
    
*   Replace **euro cylinders, mortice locks, night latches and deadlocks**
    
*   Upgrade old or worn locks to improve security
    
*   Ensure locks meet **home insurance requirements**
    

### Lock changes after incidents

*   Rapid lock changes after **lost or stolen keys**
    
*   Replace locks after a **break-in or attempted burglary**
    
*   Secure doors where keys may have been copied
    
*   Temporary fixes available if parts need ordering

*   **No call-out charges** — honest, upfront pricing
    
*   **DBS-checked engineers** for your safety
    
*   Care taken to **avoid unnecessary damage**
    
*   **12-month guarantee on parts and labour** (where applicable)

Everything You Need to Know Before You Call

[Contact us](/contact-us/)

### [How quickly can you change my locks?](#)

We aim to provide **same-day service** wherever possible, with 1-hour emergency response available in many areas.

### [Are the new locks insurance-approved?](#)

Yes. We install **BS3621-certified locks** and 3-star cylinders approved by most UK insurance providers.

### [Do I need to change all the locks?](#)

Not always. We’ll assess whether a **re-key** or full lock replacement is best for security and cost-effectiveness.

### [Can you upgrade to a higher-security lock?](#)

Absolutely. We stock a wide range of **anti-snap, anti-drill, and smart locks** to suit different door types.

### [Can I send you a picture of my lock first?](#)

Absolutely — we encourage it. You can send a photo of your lock via **WhatsApp or our contact form**, and we’ll assess it quickly to give you an accurate quote or confirm if we can carry out the work in a single visit. It helps us bring the right parts and avoid unnecessary delays.

### [Do you charge call-out fees?](#)

No — we **don’t charge any call-out fees**. You only pay for the work we carry out. Our pricing is transparent, and we’ll always provide a clear quote before starting any job, with no hidden extras. Day or night, you won’t be hit with surprise charges just for showing up.
