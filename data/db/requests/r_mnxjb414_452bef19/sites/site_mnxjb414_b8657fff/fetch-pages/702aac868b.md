Everything You Need to Know Before You Call

[Contact us](/contact-us/)

### [Do you offer commercial locksmith services outside of London?](#)

Yes. While we’re based in London, we also cover the Home Counties and extend our service up to Huntingdon. Our mobile locksmiths are strategically located for fast business callouts.

### [Can you help if we’re locked out of our office or warehouse?](#)

Absolutely. We offer 24/7 emergency lockout assistance for all commercial properties. A qualified locksmith can usually be on-site within 30 minutes, with no call-out charge.

### [What types of commercial locks do you work with?](#)

We handle all major commercial locking systems including euro cylinders, mortice locks, digital locks, access control systems, shutter locks and master key suites. We only fit high-quality, insurance-approved components.

### [Do you install access control or keyless entry systems?](#)

Yes. We supply and install a range of digital and keyless systems to suit businesses of all sizes—from standalone coded locks to fully integrated multi-door access control with staff permissions.

### [Are your locksmiths vetted and qualified?](#)

All our locksmiths are DBS-checked, trained, and fully insured. We do not outsource work to unverified contractors—every job is handled by our trusted in-house team.

### [Can you provide maintenance or scheduled servicing for business locks?](#)

Yes. We offer ongoing support, including lock maintenance, security audits and scheduled servicing for multi-site businesses or high-traffic premises. Talk to us about setting up a tailored service agreement.
