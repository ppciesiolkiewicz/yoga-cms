### [Can you open a safe if I’ve lost the key or forgotten the code?](#)

Yes — our safe engineers use **non-destructive entry methods** where possible to open locked safes. Whether it’s a lost code, jammed bolt, or broken key, we’ll regain access quickly and safely.

### [What types of safes can you work on?](#)

We service a wide range of safe types including **home safes, office safes, deposit safes, underfloor safes, fireproof safes**, and **digital keypad or combination safes**.

### [Do you replace or upgrade safe locks?](#)

Absolutely. We can **repair or upgrade mechanical and digital locks**, including keypads, dials, and biometric access systems — bringing your safe up to modern security standards.

### [Will opening the safe damage it?](#)

In most cases, we use **specialist non-destructive techniques**, especially for high-end or antique safes. If drilling is required, we always minimise impact and offer repair or lock replacement after entry.

### [Is your safe locksmith service confidential?](#)

Yes. All work is carried out **with discretion, professionalism, and confidentiality**. Our engineers are **DBS-checked** and trained to respect privacy at all times.

### [Can you remove or dispose of an old safe?](#)

Yes — we offer **safe removal and disposal** services, including heavy-duty safes that require specialist equipment. We’ll assess site access and provide a secure removal plan.
