Everything You Need to Know Before You Call

[Contact us](/contact-us/)

### [Can you get me back into my house if I’m locked out?](#)

Yes — we specialise in **non-destructive entry** to help you regain access without damaging your lock or door, in most cases.

### [How fast can you arrive in an emergency?](#)

Our typical response time is **30–60 minutes**, depending on your location in London or the surrounding counties. We’re available **24/7, 365 days a year**.

### [Will your service damage my door or lock?](#)

No — we always aim to use **non-destructive methods** first. Our engineers are trained in safe techniques and carry specialist tools to protect your property.

### [What happens if my lock is broken or damaged?](#)

We carry a full range of **British Standard locks** and can replace your lock on the spot if needed — fully compliant with home insurance policies.

### [Are your locksmiths trustworthy?](#)

Yes — all our technicians are **DBS-checked**, uniformed, and trained to handle emergency situations professionally and with discretion.

### [Can you help at night or on weekends?](#)

Absolutely — we’re available **24/7 including nights, weekends and holidays**, because we know lockouts don’t follow business hours.
