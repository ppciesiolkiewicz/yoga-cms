If you are locked out or indeed locked in our expert locksmiths will normally use non destructive entry techniques, such as lock sliping, lock picking and make use of the many tools our locksmiths are highly skilled and trained in to gain access to your property or to get to you when you are locked in.

All our locksmiths vans carry high levels of stock including a wide range of locks and keys. So in most situations we can carry out, the repair or installation on our first visit.
