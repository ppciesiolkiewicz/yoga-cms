# About

Why choose our locksmiths?

## Your Local team of Locksmiths In London.

When you call us, Alan, Dimitri or Ashley will be there straight away to help you. We drive vans with our company name on the side so you know who we are. Our Locksmiths are all members of the Master Locksmith Association (the MLA is the industry body for Locksmiths which vets and accredits Master Locksmiths). Master Locksmiths are more skilled and have been trained to a higher standard than non-Master locksmiths (very few of the locksmiths you'll find on Google are Master Locksmiths). We are a Limited company answerable to the Master Locksmiths Association should something ever go wrong.

Alan Sharpe is the owner and principal locksmith at London Locksmiths. He has been a Locksmith for nearly 35 years having started out as a trainee in 1981 in a lock shop in Brixton. He trained in the shop for 5 years learning all aspects of lock picking, key cutting and of course customer service. Alan was promoted and trained for a further 5 years learning lock fitting, emergency professional lock opening and all the other aspects of locksmithing. He gained his Master Locksmith qualification in 1998.

Dimitri is Alan's chief locksmith and has worked for the company for 15 years. Ashley arrived at London Locksmiths straight from school at 16 and was trained in-house. He has been working for London Locksmiths for ten years now.
