## FAQ

![Yoga Berlin](https://images.squarespace-cdn.com/content/v1/670a3246fc366f0647044a2d/38423dae-81d5-4576-9316-feb208fc0a3b/_DSC9292.jpg)

- #### HOW DO I BOOK AND PAY FOR A CLASS AT SHA-LA STUDIOS?






Please ensure you book and pay for your class online. SHA-LA Studios operates cashless for your convenience. Most of our classes are available on ClassPass, Urban Sports Club, WellHub & WellPass. Please see schedule.

- #### WHEN DO THE DOORS OPEN FOR CLASSES?






Doors open 15 minutes before class starts. We kindly ask that you arrive on time, as late entries are not permitted to respect the flow of the class.

- #### IS EQUIPMENT AVAILABLE IN THE STUDIO?






Yes! At SHA-LA, we provide all the necessary yoga equipment, including mats and props, to enhance your practice. Please remember to clean your mat thoroughly after use.

- #### ARE CLASSES TAUGHT IN ENGLISH?






Yes, all classes at SHA-LA are taught in English.

- #### WHAT IF I'M LATE FOR CLASS?






We recommend arriving 15 minutes early to fully immerse yourself in your practice. If you do arrive late, please note that late entries are not permitted once the class has started to ensure respect for all participants.

- #### WHAT IS THE CANCELLATION POLICY?






The cancellation deadline for all studio classes is 12 hours before the start time. Cancellations can be made through your customer account on your account. If you cancel in time, one credit (access to a class) will be returned to your account. Please note that payouts and refunds are not possible, and we cannot accommodate email requests for last-minute cancellations.

- #### I FORGOT SOMETHING AT THE STUDIO. WHAT SHOULD I DO?






If you’ve left something behind, please reach out to us via email at [hello@shalastudios.com](mailto:hello@shalastudios.com) as soon as possible. To expedite the process, include a description of the item and any relevant details, such as the date and time of your last visit.
