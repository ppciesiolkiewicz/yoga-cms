#### SHALA – Sanskrit: शाला śālā,  means "Home".

_"Yoga Shala" is the home in which yoga is practiced and experienced._

SHA-LA Berlin is the sanctuary where your practice evolves, learning stays embodied, and community is at the center: Exceptional yoga and pilates classes. 1:1 treatments for deep recovery. The SHA-LA Academy for immersive learning.

Whether you come to move, restore, or deepen your knowledge _—_ **_THIS Must be your place_** _._
