# ABOUT

## **FLOW LIKE YOU MEAN IT!**

At SHA-LA, we offer a space for holistic wellness in Prenzlauer Berg and Kreuzberg, Berlin. It’s more than a yoga studio—it’s a place to connect through yoga, sound baths, breathwork, Pilates, and more. Our goal is to support your well-being by creating a space where movement and mindfulness come together.

With a team of over 90 skilled teachers, we honor traditional yoga practices while embracing diverse styles to suit every body and every experience level.

Our hands-on approach is designed to enhance your practice and help you feel supported throughout your journey.

Our studios provide mats, props, and lockers for our guests. Our lockers follow a "bring your own lock" policy, so feel free to bring a lock with you for added convenience. Additionally, both of our studios are equipped with security doors to ensure a safe and uninterrupted experience during classes.

### **JOANA REICHERTS**

### **SHAHED NAJI**

**Joana Reicherts and Shahed Naji** are the driving forces behind SHA-LA Studios. Having experienced the profound positive impact of yoga on their own well-being, they are passionate about creating a space that shares these benefits with the broader community.

Believing in the power of lifelong learning, Joana and Shahed strive to cultivate a yogic environment where participants are guided by experienced teachers with inspiring stories and diverse backgrounds. For them, SHA-LA is a community-focused space that empowers individuals on their journeys rather than placing the teachers at the center.
