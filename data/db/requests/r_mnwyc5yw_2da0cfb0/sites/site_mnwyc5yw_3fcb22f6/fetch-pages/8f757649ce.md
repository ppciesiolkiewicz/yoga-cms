# The Sha-La Academy

#### **Immersive, Practice-Led Learning for Teachers & Practitioners**

The **SHA-LA Academy** is a space for deep, embodied learning where yoga teachers and dedicated practitioners refine their skills, deepen their knowledge, and reconnect with the foundations of their practice.

Our **yoga teacher trainings and continuing education programmes in Berlin** are grounded in experience and designed to support long-term development beyond certification.

We also host **internationally respected teachers**, including **Ambra Vallo, Miles Mortensen, Rebecca Hannah, Ben Kalra, and Sarah Powers**, bringing advanced yoga education and global perspectives to the Berlin yoga community.
