Miranda Li

Lead teacher Hatha

Miranda has over 15 years of experience teaching Hot Yoga, including Bikram Yoga, Hatha Yoga, Vinyasa Yoga and Pilates, teaching in Hong Kong, the United States and Barcelona.

As she is also a certified massage therapist, she became an expert in anatomy and alignment, driven by the fascinating correlation between mind and body, and all its subtle ways of expressing.

She is well known for her eye for details, technique and for her unique way of guiding students with the help of physical adjustment.

Leo Cuda

Lead teacher Vinyasa

Leo is an International Yoga Teacher, Ayurveda Nutritionist, Physical Education Teacher, Therapeutic Thai Massage and Buddisht & Advaita Meditator.

Settled in Barcelona, he has spent over a decade deepening his studies and spreading the sciences of yoga around the world under the guidance of direct disciples of great masters of Yoga.

His development as a practitioner & teacher has been influenced by his studies in Physical Education, Sports Training and Nutrition. This allows him to deliver clear lectures for a greater understanding of where Western methods to achieve and maintain prime physical health are linked with the Eastern holistic sciences of Yoga.
