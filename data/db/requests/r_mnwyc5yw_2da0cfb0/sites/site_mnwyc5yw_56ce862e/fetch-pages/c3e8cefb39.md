##### A few requirements we all follow:​

- ##### Reserve your spot prior to class. You can register and sign up for our classes by using the Momence app

- ##### Classes will be open for booking 14 days prior to class.

- ##### Classes start on time. Any cancellation must be done 4 hours prior to class. If not, you will be charged for a regular class from your membership. If you are an unlimited membership holder the penalty is €10.

- ##### There are 10 spots on the waitlist, if someone cancels you will automatically be accepted into the class, if you can't attend make sure you cancel asap.

- ##### You always need to have a yoga mat and a yoga towel on top of the mat for your practice, this is for rent at reception as well.

- ##### Reception closes 5 minutes before the class. No check-in will be allowed after so please be on time.

- ##### Reception is open 30 minutes before and after class.

- ##### Payments and memberships are non-refundable as per T&Cs.


##### Thank you for your understanding and cooperation! In case of any doubts, please contact us by email or whatsapp.
