# Yoga Lotos Berlin - Yoga In Friedrichshain

## YOGA IN BERLIN

At **LOTOS** Yoga, we believe in creating opportunities for growth—both on and off the mat. That’s why we have **3 studios** across Berlin, offering over 150 classes per week in English and German, covering a wide range of styles: Vinyasa, Hatha, Yin, Power Yoga, Yoga Nidra, Meditation, Sound Healing, and more.

Our **Yoga Teacher Training** programs (200h & 500h) and continued education courses are designed not only to deepen your practice, but also to support our graduates in stepping confidently into teaching. Many of our teachers started their journey here, and our studios provide the space to grow, share, and connect with the community.

Whether you are a beginner or an advanced yogi, **LOTOS** Yoga offers guidance to help you flow, learn, and thrive—both in-studio and online.

**3 studios. One community. Infinite opportunities.**

## FAQ

Do you offer Yoga mats or do I have to bring my own?

We have enough yoga mats and Props for you and you can use them for free. Of course you can bring your own yoga mat if you prefer.

Can I take a trial class?

Yes, of course. You can take a trial class for 13 Euros or a 3 times trial card for only 33 Euros so you can try different classes. – [https://yoga-lotos.de/prices/](https://yoga-lotos.de/prices/)

Do I need to book my class in advance?

It is best to book your class in advance in order to reserve your place. In case you can’t book your class, get in touch with our Team per Whats App 03091544928. The will help you with the booking. – [https://yoga-lotos.de/classes/](https://yoga-lotos.de/classes/)

Do you have more than one location?

Yes, we have 2 locations (Mainzer Strasse 21 and Scharnweberstraße 30) both in Friedrichshain.

Both studios are very close to each other anyway we advise you to check in which studio is your next class to make sure you go to the right location – [https://yoga-lotos.de/locations/](https://yoga-lotos.de/locations/)

Do you offer Online Classes?

Yes, all of our in-studio classes are simultaneously streamed online – [https://yoga-lotos.de/livestream-stundenplan/](https://yoga-lotos.de/livestream-stundenplan/)

How do I get the link to my online class?

Once you have registered for the online class you will receive the link 15 minutes before the class starts – [https://yoga-lotos.de/livestream-stundenplan/](https://yoga-lotos.de/livestream-stundenplan/)

Do you offer classes in English?

Yes, we have a large number of classes in English as well as in German – [https://yoga-lotos.de/classes/](https://yoga-lotos.de/classes/)

Which kind of Yoga classes do you have?

We have a wide variety of yoga classes from classical Hatha Yoga to more challenging and dynamic forms of yoga such as Vinyasa.

Also more tranquil and meditative forms such as Yin Yoga or Restorative as well as Healthy Back Yoga classes to improve your posture and alleviate those pains that affect so many nowadays. You can also practice Meditation, Yoga Nidra, Pranayama and much more!

We also offer [Pre & Post Natal Yoga](https://yoga-lotos.de/pregnancy-yoga/) well as [Health Insurance supported courses.](https://yoga-lotos.de/krankenkasseen/)  – If you are interested in studying Yoga or learning it in a more in-depth way you will also find many possibilities in our [Yoga Academy.](https://yoga-lotos.de/academy/)

Do you offer courses supported by health insurance?

Yes, we have several courses on different topics supported by health insurance – [https://yoga-lotos.de/krankenkasseen/](https://yoga-lotos.de/krankenkasseen/)

Do you offer Yoga Teacher Training?

Yes, in our Yoga Academy we offer teacher training courses as well as a vast number of postgraduate courses for teachers on exciting topics. We also offer possibilities for new teachers to gain experience and develop professionally [– https://yoga-lotos.de/academy/](https://yoga-lotos.de/academy/)

Can I buy Yoga items in your studio?

Yes, in our Yoga Shop in the studio at Mainzer Strasse 21 you will find many items such as Yoga Mats, Props, Singing Bowls, books, essential oils, and more.

You can also check our Online Shop and buy online and we will send you the item of your choice right away – [https://shop-lotos.com](https://shop-lotos.com/)
