## THE ART OF BHAKTI YOGA: **THREE BOONS 15+HR MANTRA & HARMONIUM TRAINING**

Embark on a journey of the heart in this 15+ hour Mantra & Harmonium Training. Experience the transformative power of your own voice as you explore the art of chanting and learn to accompany yourself on the harmonium. Rooted in the tradition of Bhakti Yoga within our Jivamukti Yoga lineage at Three Boons Yoga Berlin, this training offers a space to deepen your connection with sacred sound, breath, and devotion.

**The Three Boons 15+hour Mantra&Harmonium Training is especially for you:**

- if you want to connect more deeply with your voice and the heart of Bhakti Yoga

- if you’re curious about sacred sound and the healing potential of mantra

- if you’d like to bring more devotion, presence, and musicality into your yoga practice or teaching

- if you long to be part of a community of like-minded practitioners and lovers of kīrtan


### **WHAT ARE THE PREREQUISITES TO TAKE PART?**

None :)

While this 15+HR Mantra&Harmonium Training serves also serves as continuing education for yoga teachers, it is open and beneficial for everyone.

If you have a harmonium or consider to buy one anyway, it would be great if you brought it to the training so you can train on your personal instrument. In this case, the training fee is also considerably lower (270 EUR instead of 333 EUR).

### **WHAT CAN I EXPECT FROM THE TRAINING?**

This training will guide you through the fundamentals of harmonium playing, chords, rhythm and voice activation, while you'll explore ancient mantras and their energies. Gain the confidence to chant mantra, to integrate your voice in your classes and to lead Kirtan in any space. Whether you're new to chanting mantras or already familiar with them, our Mantra&Harmonium training warmly welcomes you.

**This training includes:**

- Harmonium Basics – learn how to navigate the instrument and accompany your voice

- Music Fundamentals – learn how to play and understand simple chords and rhythm

- Voice Activation – practices to awaken, free, and strengthen your voice

- Mantra Practice – immersion in sacred chants, pronunciation, and repetition

- Bhakti Philosophy – learn the stories and symbolism of the devas & devis

- Breath Awareness – using breath as a bridge between body, voice, and devotion

- Rhythm & Flow – learning the basics of tempo and how to stay connected to it while chanting

- Confidence Building – support in integrating mantra into your teaching or practice

- Kīrtan Participation – the chance to share a mantra with the group during our closing community kīrtan

- Connection & Sangha – time to chant, learn, and grow in a supportive circle

- Certification – receive a certificate of completion for your hours of study and practice to prove your continuing education as a yoga teacher

**Michelle Longreé**

is an Advanced Certified 800+ HR Jivamukti Yoga teacher and devoted Bhakti practitioner based in Berlin. Her journey into yoga began as a way to balance a high-paced corporate life—but it was when she encountered the Jivamukti method in 2016 that she truly found her path. Deeply drawn to its focus on compassion, sacred sound, and devotion, Michelle has been immersed in Bhakti Yoga ever since.

She completed her foundational 200-hour Vinyasa teacher training in 2018, followed by a 50-hour Bhakti immersion in 2019, and the 300-hour Jivamukti Teacher Training at the Govardhan Eco Village in India in 2020 with Jules Febre and Rima Rabbath, mentored by Martyna Eder. In 2023 Michelle went through the Three Boons Yoga Berlin Mentoring Program with Rowena Johnson as her mentor and then took & passed the Jivamukti Advanced Board in May 2024.

Michelle is passionate about the healing and transformative power of mantra and voice. She regularly leads Kirtans, Bhakti immersions, and Harmonium workshops in Berlin, where she creates a welcoming and supportive space for all to connect with their hearts through sound. Her classes and immersions are grounded in years of dedicated personal practice and inspired by the living tradition of our Jivamukti Yoga lineage here at Three Boons.

With her warm presence, love for the harmonium, and deep reverence for sacred chant, Michelle is uniquely gifted at helping students discover their own voice and confidence in leading mantra and kīrtan. Whether you're new to Bhakti or already on the path, her teachings offers a gentle yet powerful invitation into the heart of devotion.
