## THE ART OF TEACHING YOGA I: **THREE BOONS 180+HR YOGA TEACHER TRAINING**

If you feel a strong connection to the teachings of yoga and you want to explore this in more depth, while also learning how to share with others what you feel in your heart, then our Three Boons Yoga Teacher Training will support you on your quest.

**The Three Boons 180+hour Yoga Teacher Training is especially for you:**

- if you have sincere connection to the teachings of yoga and you want to explore them more deeply

- if you want to be able to share what you feel in your heart with others, by teaching yoga

- if you want to immerse yourself deeply in teaching yoga and/or refresh your teaching skills by

  - deepening your own practice

  - studying yoga philosophy and Sanskrit

  - learning to create meaningful yoga class sequences that are serving the purpose of yoga

  - developing clear communication skills that support the ultimate goal of the yoga practices

  - working intensely on yoga assists

### **WHAT ARE THE PREREQUISITES TO TAKE PART?**

You should be passionate about your yoga practice and you should have practiced yoga regularly for quite some time before the start of your teacher training at Three Boons Yoga. During the training, you should be able and willing to have a daily yoga practice at Three Boons Yoga Berlin (on-site or online) and take part attentively in all the sessions of the training.

### **WHAT CAN I EXPECT FROM THE TRAINING?**

Taking a yoga training like ours, will take your own yoga practice and your yoga experience to the next level. Yoga foremost is an experiential science, so this training, along with your regular practice of yoga, will eventually enable you to share your passion for yoga with others, be it by teaching yoga classes or by being more skilled to deal with life and all its multiple situations.

**Our intention with the Three Boons 180+hour Yoga Teacher Training - The Art of Teaching Yoga I is for participants to:**

- create a strong and deeply rooted foundation in the depth of your own Being; by continually working on integrating the teachings of yoga in your own life

- establish a solid foundation for becoming the best possible yogi and yoga teacher

- study and understand the core yoga philosophy and Sanskrit teachings essential for this goal

- be able to teach effectively and safely assist a 75 minutes set sequence yoga class

- understand the asanas and key yoga class sequencing principles, thus building a foundation for potentially creating your own classes/sequences

- learn about the key yoga anatomy foundations of the asanas and sequencing of yoga classes

- receive a signed official certificate confirming you successfully completed 180 hours of Yoga Teacher Training at Three Boons Yoga Berlin.

On the schedule below, the times for Wednesdays are 17:00-20:00. **The extra hour from 17:00 - 18:00** is not mandatory to take part in every week but helpful. Here we practice and work on assists with the mentors.

Practicing asana regularly (5 times a week) at Three Boons Yoga Berlin (on site or through the live-stream classes with your camera on) is required throughout the entire duration of the training (April and May 2027).

### **WHY 180+HOURS?**

From our long-time experience with teacher trainings and newly certified yoga teachers, the ideal foundation to become a solid yoga teacher are 300+ combined hours of foundational training complemented by a close mentorship period with a mentor yoga teacher (for a total combined certification of around 1,000 hours).

It is often said that it takes 10,000 hours of intensive practice to begin to master complex skills and materials. This definitely also applies to the path of becoming an accomplished yoga teacher. With combined approx. 1,000 hours of guided training, we feel new teachers will have a solid foundation to deepen their further path mainly through their own personal efforts, commitment and dedication.

As every yogi and aspirational yoga teacher follows his/her own path, we offer two volumes of small-group training (a.k.a. The Art of Teaching Yoga I and II) for a combined foundational 335+hour teacher training and, in addition, a one-on-one 630+hour mentorship program (a.k.a. The Art of Teaching Yoga III). This three step approach allows you to create your own mix-and-match path on your way to achieve the best training as a yogi and a yoga teacher:

- **180+HOUR CERTIFICATION: Our The Art of Teaching Yoga I** training is a 180+hour training that is aimed at enabling you to teach the Three Boons The Razor’s Edge 75 minute yoga class. You will receive a 180+hour certificate of completion if you actively took part in all sessions of the training.

- **200+HOUR CERTIFICATION: An option to teach a test class** in front of members of the 3BY teacher training faculty is offered to all 180+hour Three Boons teacher training participants after completion of The Art of Teaching Yoga I and 20+hours of additional self-study, practicals and preparation. Upon passing the test class exam, you will receive a 200+hour certificate from Three Boons Yoga Berlin that authorizes you to teach. The exam fee for the first set of test classes (one recorded video test class and one in person in front of the TT faculty) is already included in the 180+hour tuition. This optional process for receiving 200+HR authorization includes

  - properly preparing your test class

  - trial teaching sessions to your fellow trainees or other friends

  - producing and sending in a video of yourself teaching the Three Boons The Razor’s Edge75 yoga class to a fellow trainee/friend as a pre-requisite to be admitted to holding the in-person test class exam

  - receiving constructive feedback from our TT faculty on your video test class so you are ready to teach and pass the in-person test class

  - teaching the in-person test class to our TT faculty and other guests and students

  - receiving feedback on your in-person test class from our TT faculty right after teaching it

  - upon passing you receive a new 200+HR certificate of authorization from Three Boons Yoga Berlin
- **350+HOUR CERTIFICATION: The Art of Teaching Yoga II** is a second teacher training offering in the form of a 153+hour yoga teacher training that will enable you to deepen your studies and understanding of yoga and, combined with our 180+hour training, achieve a 333+hours certification.

- **980+HOUR CERTIFICATION: The Three Boons Yoga 630+hour Mentoring Program** can be endeavored by you after having taken a Three Boons (The Art of Teaching Yoga I or II) or a Jivamukti Yoga training and upon acceptance by a mentor teacher at our yoga school. (more information can be found [on the 630+ hour Mentoring Program section of our website](https://www.threeboonsyoga.de/630hrmentoring)).

- **COMBINATION WITH OTHER TRAININGS: If you already took other trainings before or are planning to do so later,** you could choose to pick our 180+hour training or our 153+hour training as an continuing education or a foundational training. Both trainings are a great way to dive deeper into how we teach at Three Boons Yoga Berlin, to continue your education as a yoga teacher and to qualify you to take part in our 630+hours mentoring program.  The Art of Teaching Yoga II is also very well suited to be combined with 75-hour foundational trainings, especially ones that are Jivamukti/Vinyasa Yoga based, in order to achieve a combined 200+hour certification.

### **HOW DO I PREPARE FOR THE 180+HOUR TEACHER TRAINING?**

Please make use of the unlimited yoga classes at Three Boons Yoga Berlin that you will receive after signing up for the training.

We also ask you to complete the following reading requirements and write the following essays and elaborations on your readings. This should be done before the beginning of the training. Please bring a print-out of your work to the first day of the training:

**1.** Read " _Essence of the Upanishads_" by Eknath Easwaran and write a review of the chapter of the book that inspired you the most.

**2.** Read Eknath Easwaran version of _The Bhagavad Gita._ Elaborate on three teachings that are given in the text that you find inspirational for your own spiritual practice.

**3\.** Read " _Jivamukti Yoga - Practices For Liberating Body and Soul_" by Sharon Gannon and David Life. Write a short review of each chapter. In your reviews elaborate on two or three points from each chapter that you found especially interesting and inspiring.

**4\.** Read " _Yoga and Veganism: The Diet of Enlightenment_" by Sharon Gannon and write a review of the chapter of the book that moved you the most.

Try to work on these assignments right from the moment you sign up so the material can settle in and you can incorporate it into your daily yoga practice well until the start of the training.
