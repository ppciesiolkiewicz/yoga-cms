## HERE IS HOW THE ADMIN SIDE WORKS

Sign up a at least a few minutes before the class starts to take part in our Live-Stream and we will send you the Zoom link for the class before the class starts. If you did not get it, check your spam folder for our email and make sure to check the email account that you used when signing up. Also a good idea (especially if you are on web.de or hotmail.com) to tell your email provider that info@jivamuktiberlin.de and hello@fitgrid.com are not spam (or add these emails to your contacts)..
