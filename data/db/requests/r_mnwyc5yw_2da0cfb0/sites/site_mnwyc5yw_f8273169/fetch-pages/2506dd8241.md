- Three Boons Yoga Retreats are a sacred pause, a chance to step away from the noise of daily life and to return to what is essential. We create spaces where you can reconnect with yourself through deep yoga practice rooted in our Jivamukti Yoga method, nourishing food, and beautiful handpicked locations.




















Surrounded by nature and freed from the busyness of everyday life, these settings support the unfolding of inner stillness and clarity.



To retreat means to step back, not to escape, but to turn inward. Through daily sessions of āsana, prāṇāyāma, chanting, and meditation, you reconnect with your own natural balance and strength. For some, it is a way to rekindle a practice that has been lost; for others, an opportunity to deepen an already steady path. Either way, it is a time to listen more closely, to move with awareness, and to let the practice carry you into a natural state of harmony and peace.



Each retreat offers space for reflection, study, and contemplation. We explore not only the physical aspects of yoga but also its philosophical roots. Pure, sattvic, vegan meals nourish both body and mind, supporting clarity, vitality, and ease.



Equally important is the community, people from all walks of life who share a common longing for truth and depth. In practicing and living together, we cherish our shared humanity and strengthen the quiet joy of connection.



When you return home from a Three Boons Yoga Retreat, you bring back more than memories. You bring back a renewed sense of being, a quiet strength born from deep inner work. You return not as someone who has stepped away from life, but as someone more intimately connected and realigned with it, able to carry this depth of awareness into every moment of the day.



May our practice continue to deepen.
