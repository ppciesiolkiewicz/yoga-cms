### **SPECIAL LOW INCOME/STUDENT OFFER:**  **GET 20% MORE!**

#### Since 2020, our special offer for university students, yogis with low income and Jivamukti Yoga teachers is done in kind (as it should be;).

Just write us an email to [info@threeboonsyoga.de](mailto:info@threeboonsyoga.de?subject=Request%20for%20Low%20Income%20Extra%20Yoga) within 7 days of your purchase of a class pack, unlimited card or 5th single class with a proof of your low income and we will extend any class pack/unlimited card/single class purchases by 20%, meaning:

- **5class pack:** Get 20% more visits (=6) as a student/with low income.

- **10class pack:** Get 20% more visits (=12) as a student/with low income.

- **25class pack:** Get 20% more visits (=30) visits as a student/with low income.

- **One month Unlimited:** Get 20% more days (=36) as a student/with low income.﻿﻿

- **Single Classes:** Write us after you bought your 5th single class within 3months and get another free one on us as a student/with low income.

- **Flat-Rate Contracts:** Unfortunately, we cannot give addtl. discounts/in kind extras for our new Flat-Rate Contracts at this point. As you will see, the price of as low as 58.95 EUR per month for everyone is already very affordable, less than what you can get almost anywhere else with any discount.


The beauty of this new system is that now we have the time to check every case individually from the office so that everyone below 1080€ net income per month can apply, whereas before, on-site in the studios, we could only accept formalized IDs like Berlin Pass and student IDs.

In your email requesting the student/low income 20% extra classes/days, please include a proof of eligibility such as:

- **Student ID:** you must be 33 years or younger and a regular student (Promotionsstudenten do not count!) at a state-recognized, real university or school (not just language school or Fernuni); the student ID has to have an expiration date on it

- **Berlin Pass:** Needs to show your full name as well as the expiration date.

- **ALG I or II/Hartz IV/Bürgergeld-Bescheid:** Needs to show income lower than 1.080 EUR net per month and cover the current month.

- ‍﻿﻿‍ **Steuerbescheid:** Needs to show income lower than 1.080 EUR net per month and cannot be older that 11 months.

- **Payroll/Lohnabrechnung:** Needs to show income lower than 1.080 EUR net per month, cannot be older than 1 month and we need some kind of proof that this is your only income.

- **Ukrainian passport:** Also in 2026, all Ukrainians are granted low-income benefits, no questions asked. Just need a pic of your passport. Also, we still offer one free class in Russian Sundays 9:20 taught by our Belarussian/Ukrainian expat teachers.

- **Other reasonable documents from third parties:** These testify that your income is less than 1.080 EUR net per month (for instance letter from your accountant).

- **Link to the Jivamukti Global website** with your profile as a Certified Jivamukti Yoga teacher (300hours or more)
